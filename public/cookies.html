What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, mobile phone) when you visit certain web pages. They are used to remember your preferences, help you navigate between pages efficiently, and generally improve your user experience. Cookies can also help ensure that adverts you see online are more relevant to you.
